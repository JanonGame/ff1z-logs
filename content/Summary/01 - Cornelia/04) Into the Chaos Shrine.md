# The Dark Crystal

The group pushes forward and proceeds to investigate the location. Altan disappears into some vines, investigating a secret passage.

> [!dlg-iomene] Iomene
>
> "Take care, you don't know which kinds of creepy crawlies are living in those vines."

> [!dlg-luthien-r] Luthien
> "Shhhh, I hear something very distracting."

The distraction turns out to be Bombs, which Iomene immediately wanders close enough to attract. Luthien, momentarily beside herself, shouts across the room at her.

> [!dlg-luthien] Luthien
> "YOU OLD CRONE I LET YOU OUT OF VIEW FOR 5 SECONDS!"

> [!dlg-iomene-r] Iomene
> "Language.
>
> ...Some assistance would be welcome, though."

The Bombs are put to sleep and dispatched. In the same room, sitting on a pedestal surrounded by black lightning, is a large crystalline egg radiating dark energy. They determine the spells binding it are malicious in themselves and that the object seems to hold a soul of its own.

Guiscard quietly produces a rope, fashions a lasso, and steals the crystal off the pedestal.

> [!dlg-silas] Silas
> "Are you sure this is a good idea?"

> [!dlg-guiscard-r] Guiscard
> "Silas, I'm an expert. You think anybody but an expert could pull that off?"

He wraps it in rags and puts it in his bag. Altan suggests he be the one to keep it for now, given his doubts, and adds a condition: no selling it.

> [!dlg-guiscard] Guiscard
> "Ach. C'est une tragedie. A true entrepreneur kept from refining his craft for a greater good."

Luthien floats a compromise: if it turns out to be a valuable gem with no other significance, it will fetch a much better price with the right story behind it. Guiscard finds this acceptable. They move on.

# Dances with Wolves

They find Brigid crouched at the far end of a long room, very still. She spots them and immediately puts a finger to her lips. She points to herself, then up a stone boardwalk along the wall. Then she signals Altan to strike.

![[Chaos_Shrine_Temple_Area.png]]

The room is full of wolves.

Their attempted ambush doesn't go well. There are more of them than expected, they have pack tactics, and the dire wolf among them is large enough to walk off everything they threw at it. Iomene goes down mid-fight. By the time the last wolf is put down, nearly everyone is in bad shape.

> "...Are you all okay?

> Perhaps an ambush was a mistake."
