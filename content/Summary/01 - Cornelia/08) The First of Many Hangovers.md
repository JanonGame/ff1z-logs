# Distractions through Lore

They spend the afternoon in the castle library. **Lagone** is persuaded to lend them a small gold key with a black gem at its base, on the condition that the key and the locked books stay inside the library and are returned before they leave.

The collection they work through over the course of the day:

* **The blueprints.** Written in Lufenian and labeled CONFIDENTIAL, the documents contain schematics for three enormous machines: ENDYMION, SELENE, and HYPERION, all dated between five hundred and eight hundred years ago. One in particular catches Luthien's eye. It is designated the EINHANDER ASTRAEA FRS mk.II, a Lunarian assault-class fighter jet assigned to a pilot named KLUYA. The specs describe four-speed thrusters, vulcan cannons, and a manipulator arm. 

* **The Tale of the Warriors of Light.** The familiar story, but in full. A dark cloud spreads over the world: the earth decays, the winds die, the seas stagnate, the fires grow cold. Monsters arise. Then Chaos. Four warriors appear from seemingly nowhere, armed with mythril blades and crystal power, and put an end to it. Before vanishing, they leave a prophecy: two thousand years hence, when darkness comes again, so shall they.

* **Origins of Chaos by Cid of the Lufaine.** Extremely old, badly worn, and only partially legible. What can be made out describes a collapse of balance between light and darkness, with unstable energies building toward a critical point. There is mention of crystals, of humans, and of a project to seize control of the instability. The rest is smudged beyond reading.

* **A Compiled History of the Earth-Sky Wars.** A decades-long conflict between the Earth Allied Union, comprising Cornelia, Elfheim, and Lucis, and the United Sky People's Republic of the Lunarians, allied with the Lufaine. Key events include the Battle of the Big Bridge, which opened the war; the destruction of the Lucis Kingdom and the execution of King Solomon Lucis Caelum; the mass desertion of the Lufaine people to a flying city called Endymion; and the final Cornelian strike, Operation Lunar Whale, which destroyed the USPR's main forces at Selene. A ceasefire followed. No battles are documented since.

* **A Collection of Dated World Maps.** Two things stand out. On pre-war maps, a great city sits west of Melmond where there is now nothing but plains. And on a map roughly two hundred years old, a structure is marked in the sea southeast of Onrac that does not appear on any current map.

* **The Book of Trials.** Rituals for attaining power beyond kings, gated by one prerequisite: the blessing of the dragon king Bahamut. The ritual draws on one's innermost energy. Those weak of mind, body, or spirit do not survive it.


It is late afternoon by the time they return the key.
