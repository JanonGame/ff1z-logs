# Back at the Castle


The banquet is held in the castle. Three weeks of royal planning have resulted in something genuinely grand.

Guiscard makes directly for the champagne table and returns holding a glass pressed between his forearm and chest while attempting to light his pipe with the other hand. He greets Beck and Silas with a nod each as though this is a very normal thing to be doing.

Luthien finds Silas's tie slightly wrong and fixes it before he can stop her.

> [!dlg-silas] Silas
> "I could very well do it myself, you know."

Beck is at the piano with the band. Iomene drifts over and asks if she can join him. He is happy to have her. Her performance draws the attention of everyone in earshot, including, eventually, Princess Sarah at the railing of the upper floor.

This being a first to her, and being taken in by the atmosphere, Luthien decides to try some of the surprisingly fruity alcoholic beverages on offer.

# The Prophecy

Altan and Luthien head upstairs to speak with Princess Sarah, who directs them to the court mage, **Topapa**. He is a small man and seems to have been expecting them.

> [!dlg-topapa] Topapa
> "Ahh. So you two are the ones who assisted our champions at the Chaos Shrine."

Altan asks about Garland's condition. Topapa confirms what Brigid said: no heartbeat, no soul inhabiting the body, functionally dead. The mages are doing everything they can to preserve him, but the crystal holding his soul has yet to yield to any known spell.

# The Big Announcement

The room gathers. Luthien grabs two more drinks on the way over. Altan takes one of them. Guiscard has switched to black coffee in a beer stein and is quietly urging everyone to act normal.

> [!dlg-luthien-r] Luthien
> "They're very fruity!"

Her face is red. She's buzzed.

Minister Lagone steps forward, and the medals are awarded: Brigid Rubicante, who steps out rigid and straight; Silas Scarmiglione, who briefly pulls down his eyelid and sticks his tongue out at the crowd before snapping back to attention; Beck Cagnazzo, who exhales slowly as he takes his place; and Audra Highwind, shoulders back, hands clasped behind her.

Then Princess Sarah takes the podium alone.

> [!dlg-sarah] Princess Sarah
> "I spent many days alone in that Chaos Shrine, cut off from the outside world. I truly feared I would lose my life to whatever forces had taken me from my home.
>
> But there is one man who stood out more than the rest. I was told he had briefly discarded his title and hung up his sword, because he thought he had failed to protect me on the night of my kidnapping.
>
> He endured many slings and arrows. Labeled a coward. A deserter. An Onion Knight.
>
> As a child, I asked my dear mother why we use the name of that vegetable to describe both those without experience in the ways of the world, and those who excel in the face of adversity.
>
> She told me the story of a boy named Luneth. When asked how he came to be a man of such ability, wisdom, and virtue, he said: *I simply learned to eat my vegetables.*
>
> That noble vegetable, the onion, lives on as a symbol of hardships overcome, and as the mark of a true hero.
>
> And that is why I wish to present this -- the Mark of Luneth..."

> [!narration]
> To Tiz Arrior.

The room applauds. Elle, from somewhere in the audience, is applauding louder than anyone. Tiz takes one step, then another.

> [!dlg-sarah] Princess Sarah
> "I hope that, whatever your pursuits may be in the future, that you continue to wear the title 'Onion Knight' -- not with shame, but with great pride and honor."

> [!dlg-tiz-r] Tiz
>> *bows deeply*
>
> "Thank you..."

> [!dlg-sarah] Princess Sarah
> "There is one more thing I wish to share with you all.
>
> The winds carry a stench in the air as the land decays. The fires in our homes have grown colder. Many have begun to believe the end times have come.
>
> But there is a light at the end of the dark tunnel. I believe the Warriors of Light have revealed themselves on that fateful day.
>
> I wish to present to you... our Warriors of Light."

> [!narration]
> The audience applauds feverishly as Princess Sarah steps forward with four medals, each set with a different jewel.

Iomene gets a medal with a yellow jewel at its center. *The Hope that blesses the earth.*

Luthien gets a medal with a blue jewel, still holding the drink she had not quite finished. *The Devotion that turns water into the seed of life.*

Altan, making sure the cameras don't get a good view of him, gets a medal with a red jewel. *The Courage that shall keep the fire stoked and alive.*

Guiscard, who has just managed to stash his coffee so the camera doesn't catch it, gets a medal with a green jewel. *The Passion to spread your wisdom on the winds.*

> [!dlg-sarah] Princess Sarah
> "I wish the four of you the blessings of the crystals, that they may show you the way to a brighter future.
>
> May the light of the crystals be with you always."

The reporters swarm the party. They're out of the element. One of them is drunk. What follows is a barrage of questions and a line of awkward answers.
